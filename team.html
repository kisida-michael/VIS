TEAM
The Finest Builders, Designers, Engineers, & Leaders
BRHS TSA is dedicated to teaching students one of the most integral skills in the professional and technical world - leadership. To accomplish this, numerous opportunities are provided for members to hold leadership positions within the chapter. This allows students to develop the necessary communication, networking, and teamwork skills to be successful in their future endeavors.
Advisors
The Braden River TSA chapter could not function efficiently or correctly if it were not for the advisors. As the backbone of the chapter, they constantly work with members to improve themselves as future technical leaders. With their knowledge on a vast range of topics such as engineering, photography, and designing, they’re able to provide further assistance with our team leaders. Overall, each advisor brings valuable skills to the table, which make the Braden River TSA chapter a national championship-caliber team.

Officers
The officer team serves as a foundation for the chapter’s success by leading and mentoring members. Elected annually, the group consists of juniors, sophomores, and one freshman. They plan and organize the team’s activities and serve as an example of the chapter’s core values - work ethic, character, and leadership.

Regulations for the Board of Directors were recently added to our bylaws to separate the senior members from the officer team. Often, our seniors are overburdened by college applications, internships, and graduation to be fully dedicated to the chapter, making them unsuitable for the officer team. As a result, we instituted the Board of Directors, which allows our senior members to pursue leadership roles in the chapter, albeit these roles are less demanding than the prestigious officer team. Team Leads
To further facilitate the training of members, BRHS TSA appoints a leader of each team event, referred to as a “team lead.” Team leads take charge of managing their event’s project; they delegate work, create deadlines, and see to the completion and success of the event. This position teaches students the basics of managing a small team and the importance of communication and teamwork.
Members
We would have no legacy of excellence to uphold without our many talented and dedicated members. Every member of BRHS TSA develops leadership skills through their collaboration with others, eventually creating unique projects that incorporate the varying perspectives of each member. By refining this collaborative process, our chapter develops projects that reflect our collective skills and strengths, giving each member a sense of personal leadership and accountability.
