ACTIVITIES
Community First
Braden River TSA not only plays an important role within our school, but also strives to help serve our community as well. By partnering with local community service organizations, our members are able to use their leadership and technical skills to further improve our community, Being members of TSA does not only mean furthering our knowledge in the technical world, but helping others within our society.
Community Service
River Wonderland
Braden River High School hosts an event that inspires a festive spirit in a younger demographic, which is known as “River Wonderland”. During the event, the school’s clubs unite to organize festive games, decorations, and activities that take place on the BRHS campus. Our TSA chapter heavily contributes to this event, planning many activities and assisting with festive decorations to create a riveting Christmas-themed event for families in our area.

Leadership Bootcamp
The Leadership Bootcamp is a new event to be hosted annually by our chapter’s leadership team. This event was created to create new bonds and friendships between team members and give the team a jump-start into the year. Hosted at a nearby hotel, the bootcamp is divided into different sessions over three days. Some sessions may involve brainstorming, teaching topics like MLA, practicing semi-finalist interviews, or fun activities.

Community Service
Operation Santa
Over the holidays, many families in our area struggle to hold a Christmas celebration due to a lack of food and money. To assist these families, our chapter organizes a drive, collecting funding to pay for food and gifts to assist the less fortunate. Chapter members each contribute $20 toward this cause and help with food/gift shopping, wrapping, and delivering presents to the homes of families. Our goal is to give underprivileged families a chance to enjoy the festivities of the winter holidays.
