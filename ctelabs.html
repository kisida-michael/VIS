Our Labs
In any CTE, proper equipment is required to transition from concepts and ideas to the creation of real-world products and solutions. At BRHS, we have the necessary equipment to transform student learning. Our equipment is distributed across three labs, each containing tools for wood-working, milling, 3D printing, lasering, and tools for digital production such as cameras.
