CTE Activities
Braden River High School’s CTE program is dedicated to preparing students in the Engineering & Leadership Academy with certifications in real world programs. Through industry certifications in programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere, students will have a basic foundation of the software they could eventually use later on in their careers. Overall, the introduction of using these types of software allow students to broaden their horizons and apply their skills to different fields.

Photoshop
Photoshop is the first and most basic industry certification students are introduced to in first level engineering classes. This challenging, yet useful program allows students to edit images, create website mockups, and construct display effects.
Illustrator
After pursuing and completing a Photoshop certification, most students choose to master the Illustrator program. This vector graphics editing program allows for students to create professional grade projects with an extensive variety of tools.
Premiere Pro
This is a video editing program in the Adobe Suite that can be used for projects such as commercials, online videos and other film. This certification can prove useful for amateur film editors or students looking to go into film.
Word
This certification can prove to be very helpful among students, as Microsoft Word is one of the most frequently used programs for simple documents. This program is a word processing system that students can type on. The program also has a set of tools to assist when writing and saving documents.
Excel
Microsoft Excel is a software used to create spreadsheets for organizing and managing data easier. The program uses its tools to display data in rows and columns that can be organized to the user’s liking.
PowerPoint
This program allows its users to create unique presentations. The large variety of tools such as transitions, themes, and the option to add sound, video and images, can be mastered through the student certification.
InDesign
This program is the Adobe desktop publishing and typesetting software that is used to create documents such as: flyers,magazines, and books. This certification is great for students wanting to learn graphic design and business marketing techniques.
Solidworks Associate & Professional
The CSWA certification is proof of your SOLIDWORKS® expertise with cutting-edge skills that businesses seek out and reward. Each CSWP has proven their ability to design and analyze parametric parts and moveable assemblies using a variety of complex features in SOLIDWORKS software.
